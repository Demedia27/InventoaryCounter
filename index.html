  • About Counter
    This Inventory counter is designed to eliminate errors, and delay of inventory process as a result of miss count and recounting during inventory exercise.

    How to use the Counter

    • Press the counter button while counting your items. This will retain your count memory.
    • Press save button if you want to save your count. It will save your last count and show you previous counts.
    • Press clear when you are done counting.
    • Press reset if you want to clear all count memories.
